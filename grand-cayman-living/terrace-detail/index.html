    THE TERRACES

    Located in the heart of the Town Centre, The Terraces are perfect for those who wish to live in the centre of the action. A collection of 63 luxury apartments available for rent, these homes combine the best of both worlds – the conveniences of an urban lifestyle in a stunning tropical setting. The Terraces are available in fully furnished one- and two-bedroom apartments enjoying waterfront views, underground parking, 24-hour security and access to a rooftop pool.
