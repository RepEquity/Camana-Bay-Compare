    About

    Discover Camana Bay—a lively waterfront town where you can satisfy your foodie cravings, shop for all things local, and explore our weekly Local Harvest Market. Relax on a hammock, climb our 75-foot Observation Tower or enjoy the sound of kids splashing in the fountains. It all happens just a short walk from Seven Mile Beach.
