    • OLEA Sales & Presentation Centre Now Open in Camana Bay

    21 March 2019

    The OLEA Sales & Presentation Centre is now open in the heart of Camana Bay. Located in the Town Centre on Market Street, beside Activ Angels in the space previously occupied by Le Visage, the Sales Centre is the perfect location to showcase the environmentally friendly product offerings – 124 condos, townhomes and duplexes. The waterfront community will be constructed over four phases with construction commencing in April. Initial site works have already begun.

    OLEA is a joint venture between Dart Real Estate and NCB Group, two companies that share core values of sustainability and developing high-quality projects. Solar panels will provide power to the residences and common areas and the latest geothermal systems will keep owners cool while saving them money. Combined with LED lighting, energy-efficient appliances and water-conserving plumbing fixtures, the homes are as efficient and environmentally friendly as possible. Sales are well underway with homes selling briskly.

    Sales Centre visitors can enjoy a welcoming and inviting space (furnished by Design Studio Interiors in Camana Bay) to come to learn more about this new waterfront community that features the first lazy river in the Cayman Islands. Renderings of OLEA and its amenities will be on display in the window to pique interest. Visitors can explore floorplans of OLEA’s substantial offerings: 1-, 2- and 3-bedroom condos; 3- and 4-bedroom townhomes; and 3- and 4-bedroom canalfront duplexes. Visitors can also view an interior presentation for finishings and fixtures which will give them a sense of the style and quality of these exceptional homes.

    The Sales Centre will also serve as a destination where owners can connect with the project manager to confirm their selections for their homes. Samples of finishings and fixtures, including the countertops and millwork selections will soon be available to view, and a display of kitchen cabinets will be featured so potential owners can see first-hand what these will look like.

    Visitors will also discover the many benefits that come with living in a thoughtfully designed, sustainable neighbourhood that is within walking distance to the many amenities that are available at Camana Bay. Stop by the Sales Centre any time between 12 and 5 p.m. on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays; mornings will be used for purchaser presentations or for meetings by appointments, so contact the OLEA team to set up your appointment today if mornings work better for you.

    OLEA truly embodies the Caribbean lifestyle and will enable owners to experience a greatly enhanced quality of life. The community will be about neighbours getting to know one another, spontaneous gatherings, children meeting by the pool, impromptu playing – quintessential community life. Those who work at Camana Bay can even walk to work, walk home for lunch or walk to Seven Mile Beach on the weekends. Owners can visit Camana Bay by strolling or biking across the beautiful waterfront boardwalk that connects OLEA to the Town Centre.

    Buying a home at OLEA can be life changing and the Sales & Presentation Centre will enable visitors to fully understand the benefits. To ensure interested buyers can have all of their questions answered, people can contact NCB Group’s Sales & Marketing Manager, Tania Knapik, by emailing olea@camanabay.com or calling +1-345-640-OLEA with any questions.
