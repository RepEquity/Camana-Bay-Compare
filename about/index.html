    About Camana Bay

    Discover Camana Bay—a lively waterfront town where you can satisfy your foodie cravings, shop until your heart’s content and explore our weekly Local Harvest Market. Swing lazily on a hammock, climb our 75-foot Observation Tower or be delighted by the sound of kids splashing in the fountains. It all happens only a short walk from Seven Mile Beach which is easily accessible via the Rise, a pedestrianised path.

    Camana Bay was awarded the Governor’s Award for Design and Construction Excellence for its outstanding sustainable design, and two of its office buildings are built to LEED Gold standards.

    The Town Centre is home to over a dozen restaurants, cafes and speciality eateries to cater for your every craving and occasion, from gourmet coffees, home-baked pastries, healthy lunches and freshly squeezed juices to farm-to-table dinners, designer cocktails, Sunday brunches and inspiring ice creams.

    Whether you are looking for some retail therapy, to satisfy your hunger or be entertained, Camana Bay is the place to be. View our Guest Guidelines here.

    CERTIFICATIONS

    18 Forum Lane in Camana Bay has received LEED® Gold certification. Achieving certification in LEED, which stands for Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design, signifies a commitment to sustainable development and has become the construction standard in Camana Bay.

    The data centre located in Camana Bay and operated by Dart has earned the Service Organization Controls II standard accreditation. The attainment of this certification confirms that the Camana Bay data centre has achieved this industry benchmark for management controls and aligns the data centre with global technology standards.
