    • June Events at Camana Bay

    22 May 2018

    This June, Camana Bay has different activities and events for everyone in the family. If you are looking for a new book to add to your collection of reads, sign up for The Bookends Club on June 13 when the group meets to review their latest pick, The Motorcycle Diaries by Che Guevara. Books & Books will also be hosting its monthly interactive dialogue with the experts from Infinite Mindcare. If you haven’t experienced one of these insightful sessions before, stop by on Saturday, June 16 for a discussion on this month’s topic: “Can You Hear Me Now?” Effective & Efficient Communication Skills.

    We are also celebrating Father’s Day this month and if you are looking to take Dad for a meal this Father’s Day, consider KARoo’s outdoor BBQ brunch with views of the water on Sunday, June 17. Before brunch gets started, dads can take their toddlers to Cayman Music School for their Daddy & Me Toddler Music Class where they can enjoy playing instruments and have fun making music. If Dad loves to cook, treat him to one of Bon Vivant’s coooking classes, such as French Cuisine with Chef Keith Griffin on June 12 or meat lovers can attend the Carne + Vino with Chef Dylan Benoit where you will learn about cuts and how to find the perfect sauce pairing.

    At the end of the month on June 30, Camana Bay Cinema will be hosting an exclusive 3D screening of Jean-Michel Cousteau’s Secret Ocean. Take a dive and experience the beauty and diversity of an underwater world few get to encounter. The movie will be followed by a Q&A with Jean-Michel Cousteau himself. Donations of CI$5 or more are encouraged and will benefit Ocean Futures Society and the Central Caribbean Marine Institute (CCMI).

    For more real-time updates and what’s happening at Camana Bay, remember to download the Camana Bay Events App, available for download on the App Store or Google Play.

    Beginners’ Yoga Course

    Tuesdays throughout June, 10-11:30 a.m.; Thursdays throughout June, 7-8:30 p.m., Cayman Music School
    Cayman Music School is hosting a beginners’ yoga course where you will learn proper breathing including Pranayama, yoga Asana, relaxation techniques, following a proper diet, as well as positive thinking and meditation. Classes are limited to 10 people and the course costs CI$250 per person. A special discount of 30% is available for clients of Quinntessential Movement and Cayman Music School. For more details and to register, call +1.345.938.3838 or email info@caymanmusicschool.com.

    Open Yoga Course
    Tuesdays throughout June, 7-8:30 p.m.; Thursdays throughout June, 10-11:30 a.m.; Cayman Music School
    This class is open to all who have completed the Beginners’ Yoga Course as well as to practitioners of western styles of yoga (knowledge of alternate nostril breathing/anuloma viloma pranayama is needed). This open class includes pranayama, sun salutations and the 12 main asanas/poses of Sivananda yoga and their variations. The cost for 10 classes is CI$250 per person. The cost per class is CI$25 per person. Classes are limited to 10 people and pre-registration is required to attend this class. For more details and to register, call +1.345.938.3838 or email info@caymanmusicschool.com.

    Classics at the Cinema
    Tuesdays throughout June, 7 p.m., Camana Bay Cinema
    Camana Bay Cinema features a classic movie every Tuesday as part of its Classics at the Cinema series. Shown in the VIP theatre, tickets are CI$20 per person and include a small popcorn. For more details, visit bigscreen.ky/classics.

    *June 5: Dirty Harry (R)
    *June 12: E.T. Extra Terrestrial (PG)
    *June 19: Doctor Zhivago (PG-13)
    *June 26: Titanic (PG-13)

    *Movies are subject to change.

    Camana Bay Cinema’s Kids Club
    Saturdays throughout June, 10 a.m., Camana Bay Cinema
    Kids and adults can take in classic family-friendly movies with brighter lights and softer sounds every Saturday. Tickets are CI$5 per person. For more details, visit bigscreen.ky/kids-club.

    *June 2: Old Dogs (PG)
    *June 9: Matilda (PG)
    *June 16: Planes (PG)
    *June 23: Space Jam (PG)
    *June 30: Zootopia (PG)

    *Movies are subject to change.

    United We Stand: The Musical
    Saturday, June 9, 5-6:15 p.m., Cayman Music School
    Join the Bookends Club when they meet to review their latest pick, The Motorcycle Diaries by Che Guevara. For more details, call +1.345.640.2665.

    The Bookends Club
    Wednesday, June 13, 6:30 p.m., Books & Books
    Come out to Cayman Music School and watch their talented musical theatre students performing some of Broadway’s best musical numbers. To book your tickets, call +1.345.938.3838 or email info@caymanmusicschool.com.

    French Cuisine with Chef Keith Griffin
    Thursday, June 14, 6-8 p.m., Bon Vivant Kitchen Studio
    Get ready to channel your inner Julia Child. This class will focus on the methods and techniques required to cook classic French dishes – French onion soup, coq au vin and soufflé. The cost is CI$75 per person includes food tastings. To register, call +1.345.623.2665 or email info@bonvivant.ky.

    Infinite Mindcare Talk Series
    Saturday, June 16, 2-3 p.m., Books & Books
    Books & Books hosts this monthly interactive dialogue promoting awareness on different topics about mental health with the experts from Infinite Mindcare. This month’s topic is “Can You Hear Me Now?” Effective & Efficient Communication Skills. For information on this event, call +1.345.640.2665.

    Father’s Day Special: Daddy & Me Toddler Music Class
    Sunday, June 17, 11-11:45 a.m., Cayman Music School
    This Father’s Day come and bond with your children over music and giggles. Experience the twinkle in your little one’s eyes as you enjoy some great music and all types of fun instruments. This class is for children ages 18 months to 2.5 years. The cost is CI$20 if you pay in advance or CI$25 for drop-in. For more details and to register, call +1.345.938.3838 or email info@caymanmusicschool.com.

    Carne + Vino with Chef Dylan Benoit
    Tuesday, June 19, 6-8 p.m., Bon Vivant Kitchen Studio
    This class will examine the wonderful world of meat, including beef and lamb, prepared to perfection with accompanying sauces. You will learn about cuts, flavours and how to put together dazzling dishes to impress any crowd. The cost is CI$85 per person and includes food tastings. To register, call +1.345.623.2665 or email info@bonvivant.ky.

    Culture at the Cinema
    Saturday, June 23, 8 p.m., Camana Bay Cinema
    Enjoy a pre-recorded screening of The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time, in the VIP theatre. Tickets are CI$40 per person. Must be 18 years or older to attend. For more information and tickets, visit bigscreen.ky/cultureschedule.

    Exclusive 3D Screening of Jean-Michel Cousteau’s Secret Ocean
    Saturday, June 30, 10 a.m., Camana Bay Cinema
    Jean-Michel Cousteau, son of ocean pioneer Jacques Cousteau, invites viewers to dive into a whole new world that will leave you in awe of the beauty and diversity of the oceans – the source of all life on our planet. The movie will be followed by a Q&A with Jean-Michel Cousteau himself. Donations of CI$5 per person are encouraged. Proceeds will benefit Ocean Futures Society and the Central Caribbean Marine Institute (CCMI). For more information and to reserve your tickets, email caymanambassadors@ritzcarlton.com

     
    WEEKLY RITUALS

    Toddler Tuesdays
    Tuesdays: Imagination Playground 10 a.m.-7 p.m., the Crescent; and Camana Bay Story Time 11-11:30 a.m., Camana Bay Cinema
    Children can enjoy splashing in the interactive fountains at Imagination Playground and take part in a live story reading and sing along with exciting new books and beloved children’s songs with Camana Bay Story Time. These events are free and open to the public.

    Farmers & Artisans Market
    Wednesdays, 12-7 p.m., the Paseo
    Discover an array of local purveyors offering products and experiences found only in Cayman. Come early for the widest selection of fresh produce and farm goods then browse an eclectic selection of artisanal products and handicrafts.

    Open Canvas
    Wednesdays, 7 p.m., KARoo
    Every Wednesday, Open Canvas provides a public venue for local artists to display their talents and work on their latest creations. Art enthusiasts can join the gathering and watch paintings come to life one brushstroke at a time. Artists can contact douglascameron@cib.ky to reserve an easel and must bring their own art supplies. For more details, call +1.345.946.6633.

    The Flavour Tour
    Wednesdays, 7-9 p.m., West Indies Wine Company
    Savour the flavours of island life on the Camana Bay Flavour Tour! Join a Camana Bay host on a guided walking tour of our signature restaurants. Dine on small-plate courses featuring local ingredients presented in the unique styles of the participating restaurants. Begin the evening with a West Indies Wine Company wine card to enjoy the shop’s wines on tap, then experience our dining scene as your host takes you on a guided dining adventure of three Camana Bay restaurants for tantalising fare made with local ingredients. Finish off the night with a scoop of delicious sorbetto or gelato at Gelato & Co., featuring classic and new Italian creations as well as locally infused flavours, such as passionfruit and mango. The cost is US$89 per person all-inclusive. To purchase tickets, visit camanabay.com/flavour-tour. To enquire about private group bookings, please email flavourtour@camanabay.com for additional information.

    Thursday Night Live Music
    Thursdays, 6:30-9:30 p.m., KARoo
    Enjoy acoustic tunes performed live from 6:30 p.m. with happy hour specials. For more details, call +1.345.946.6633.

    Friday Night Live Music
    Fridays, 7-10 p.m., Abacus
    Enjoy contemporary Caribbean sounds at Abacus with live entertainment on Friday evenings. Stop by for drinks before catching a movie or stay for dinner and welcome your weekend in style. For more details, call +1.345.623.8282.

    Imagination Playground
    Saturdays, 10 a.m.-7 p.m., the Crescent
    Imagination Playground invites kids to build, dream and explore a new world every week in the Town Centre’s dancing fountains with giant foam blocks in all shapes and sizes. Little ones can recreate familiar objects like animals, rocket ships and more. This event is every Tuesday and Saturday and is free and open to the public.

    Sing Along Story Time
    Saturdays, 10:30 a.m., Books & Books
    Young readers four to seven years old and their parents can join the storytellers at Books & Books to sing along to some of their favourite books.

    Saturday Night Live Music
    Saturdays, 7-10 p.m., Abacus
    Enjoy relaxing tunes performed live from 7-10 p.m. as you indulge in a tapas menu and drinks or treat yourself to a dinner date. For more details, call +1.345.623.8282.

    Sunday BBQ Brunch
    Sundays, 11 a.m.-3 p.m., KARoo
    Savour a tasty selection of fresh off-the-grill favourites with all the traditional BBQ fixings for CI$30 per person and CI$12.50 for children ages 12 and under. For more details, call +1.345.946.6633.

    Dim Sum Brunch
    Sundays, 11:30 a.m.-3 p.m., Mizu Asian Bistro + Bar
    Enjoy an expanded selection of dim sum and more at Mizu Asian Bistro + Bar’s Dim Sum Brunch every Sunday. Indulge in a selection of appetisers, soups and sushi with an array of noodle, rice and main dishes. The cost is CI$49 per person all-inclusive and reservations are required at +1.345.640.0001.

    –END–
    For full event details and more Town Centre happenings, download the Camana Bay Events App available on the App Store and Google Play or visit camanabay.com/events.
