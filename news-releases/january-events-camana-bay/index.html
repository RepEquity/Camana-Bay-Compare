    • January Events At Camana Bay

    01 January 2018

    It’s a new year and Camana Bay is starting off the year by making it easier than ever for you to meet your New Year’s resolutions. Keep an eye out on our social media channels for inventive ways to meet every goal, and for chances to win. If new experiences were first on your list, we are bringing back our popular weekly events that took a break over the holidays, starting with our #AllAboutLocal Wednesdays with the Farmers & Artisans Market returning January 10 and Flavour Tour on January 17, and Toddler Tuesdays featuring Story Time at the cinema, back on January 9. Camana Bay Cinema is bringing back Kids Club every other Saturday along with a range of programming for every taste, from Classics at the Cinema to Culture at the Cinema.

    Make sure you attend LiveWell at the Arts & Recreation Centre where you can get tips on taking care of your mind, body and spirit at Grand Cayman’s ultimate health, wellness and beauty expo presented by Cayman Health. Wellness also mean taking care of your mental health and Books & Books will host this monthly interactive dialogue promoting awareness on different topics about mental health with the experts from Infinite Mindcare. This month’s topic is Healthy Habit Formation.

    This year also marks a very special year for Taste of Cayman as the foodie festival celebrates 30 years of Grand Cayman’s culinary scene. Come out and taste signature dishes and cocktails from some of the island’s best restaurants while enjoying food demos, live music and a fireworks display on the Festival Green.

    Camana Bay Cinema’s Kids Club

    Saturdays, Jan. 6 and 20, 10 a.m., Camana Bay Cinema

    Kids and adults can take in classic family-friendly movies with brighter lights and softer sounds every other Saturday. Tickets are CI$5 per person. For more details, visit bigscreen.ky/kids-club

    *Jan. 6: The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, The Witch and The Wardrobe (2005, PG)

    *Jan. 20: Enchanted (2007, PG)

    *Movies are subject to change.

    Live Well

    Saturday, Jan. 20, 12-8 p.m., Arts & Recreation Centre

    Take care of your mind, body and spirit by coming to Grand Cayman’s ultimate health, wellness and beauty fair presented by Cayman Health. Tickets are CI$10 per person and proceeds will benefit one of four charitable organisations in Grand Cayman.

     Infinite Mindcare: Healthy Habit Formation

    Saturday, Jan. 20, 2-3 p.m., Books & Books

    Books & Books hosts this monthly interactive dialogue promoting awareness on different topics about mental health with the experts from Infinite Mindcare. This month’s topic is Healthy Habit Formation. For information on this event, call +1.345.640.2665.

     Taste of Cayman

    Saturday, Jan. 27, 5-11:45 p.m., Festival Green

    This year marks the 30th anniversary of the beloved culinary event. Taste signature dishes and cocktails from some of the best restaurants on Grand Cayman while enjoying food demos, live music and a fireworks display. General admission for adults is CI$40, CI$20 for children and CI$150 for VIP tickets. For more information on this event and for tickets, visit http://www.tasteofcayman.org.

    WEEKLY RITUALS

     Toddler Tuesdays
    Tuesdays; Imagination Playground 10 a.m.-7 p.m., the Crescent and Camana Bay Story Time 11-11:30 a.m., Camana Bay Cinema

    Children can enjoy splashing in the interactive fountains at Imagination Playground and take part in a live story reading and sing along with exciting new books and beloved children’s songs with Camana Bay Story Time. These events are free and open to the public.

    Farmers & Artisans Market

    Wednesdays, 12-7 p.m., the Paseo
    Discover an array of local purveyors offering products and experiences found only in Cayman. Come early for the widest selection of fresh produce and farm goods then browse an eclectic selection of artisanal products and handicrafts.

     Open Canvas

    Wednesdays, 7 p.m., KARoo

    Every Wednesday, Open Canvas provides a public venue for local artists to display their talents and work on their latest creations. Art enthusiasts can join the gathering and watch paintings come to life one brushstroke at a time. Artists can contact douglascameron@cib.ky to reserve an easel and must bring their own art supplies. For more details, call +1.345.946.6633.

    The Flavour Tour

    Wednesdays, 7-9 p.m., West Indies Wine Company

    Experience the new format of the Flavour Tour! Whet your appetite with a sommelier-led tasting of two wines at West Indies Wine Company, then experience Camana Bay’s dining scene as your host takes you on a guided culinary adventure at four Grand Cayman restaurants for a tantalising meal. The evening will wrap up with a digestif offering at The King’s Head, a traditional English pub. The cost is US$89 per person all-inclusive. To purchase tickets, visit camanabay.com/flavour-tour

     

    Friday Night Live Music
    Fridays, 7-10 p.m., Abacus

    Enjoy contemporary Caribbean sounds at Abacus with live entertainment on Friday evenings. Stop by for drinks before catching a movie or stay for dinner and welcome your weekend in style. For more details, call +1.345.623.8282.

    Imagination Playground

    Saturdays, 10 a.m.-7 p.m., the Crescent

    Imagination Playground invites kids to build, dream and explore a new world every week in the Town Centre’s dancing fountains with giant foam blocks in all shapes and sizes. Little ones can recreate familiar objects like animals, rocket ships and more. This event is every Tuesday and Saturday and is free and open to the public.

     Story & Craft Time
    Saturdays, 10:30 a.m., Books & Books

    Young readers and their parents can join the storytellers at Books & Books for exciting new tales and crafts that inspire imagination and creativity.

    Saturday Night Buffet with Live Music
    Saturdays, 7-10 p.m., Abacus

    Enjoy Caribbean-inspired jazz performed live from 7-10 p.m. as you indulge in a buffet featuring some of Abacus’ chefs’ best creations, including options from the land and sea. For more details, call +1.345.623.8282.Sunday BBQ Brunch
    Sundays, 11 a.m.-3 p.m., KARoo
    Savour a tasty selection of fresh off-the-grill favourites with all the traditional BBQ fixings for CI$30 per person, and CI $12.50 for children ages 12 and under. Enjoy 20% off all drinks and “all-you-can-bubble” for CI$19. For more details, call +1.345.946.6633.

     Dim Sum Brunch
    Sundays, 11:30 a.m.-3 p.m., Mizu Asian Bistro + Bar
    Enjoy an expanded selection of dim sum and more at Mizu Asian Bistro + Bar’s Dim Sum Brunch every Sunday. Indulge in a selection of appetisers, soups and sushi with an array of noodle, rice and main dishes. The bar includes bubbles, house wine, domestic bottles of beer and mixed well drinks. The cost is CI$49 per person all-inclusive and reservations are required at +1.345.640.0001.
