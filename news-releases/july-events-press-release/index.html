    • July Events at Camana Bay

    19 June 2018

    Live summer at Camana Bay, where the season means movies, eating out, outdoor fun, summer camps and having a great time.

    The month kicks off with a special Constitution Day Moonlight & Movies, where families can bring their picnic blankets and enjoy the film, Disney’s Moana. The Moonlight & Movies summer series will then continue on July 10, featuring a different family favourite every Tuesday under the stars.

    Summer camps are also back, with options available for every age group and for everyone’s interest. The Multi-Sport Camp is back for its eighth year, offering aspiring athletes coaching in volleyball, basketball, tennis, swimming and football. Young athletes who have dreams of playing in the NBA also have the opportunity to sign up for the 10th Annual Camana Bay Basketball Camp, where they will learn how to hone their skills as well as meet and play with a professional basketball player from the NBA.

    If sports aren’t your style, there are camps available for the junior chefs in your life at Bon Vivant Kitchen Studio with the Budding Chef Summer Culinary Camps and the Shutterbugs Camp for the creative photographers who want to learn the art of photography from the professionals at Picture This Studios. If your children are more of the creative type then experience the Cayman Music School Camp which will feature music, singing, dance, drama, arts and crafts, yoga and karaoke.

    If you are looking for things to do in Grand Cayman this summer, it’s never been easier to live summer than with Camana Bay!

    For more real-time updates and what’s happening at Camana Bay, remember to download the Camana Bay Events App, available for download on the App Store or Google Play.

    Constitution Day Moonlight & Movies

    Monday, July 2, 7-9 p.m., the Crescent
    Moonlight & Movies is back and kicking off with a special Constitution Day showing. Families can come out to the Crescent and enjoy watching a movie under the stars, purchase food and relax and enjoy the show. The movie showing will be Moana (PG).*

    *Movie subject to change.

    8th Annual Multi-Sports Camp 2018
    July 3-6, July 9-13 and July 16-20, 8 a.m.-12 p.m.
    Camana Bay Sports Complex
    Young athletes of all levels can get active and improve their skills while having a blast at this popular multi-sport camp, including volleyball, basketball, tennis, swimming, football and more! Drop-off is available from 7:30 a.m. To register, email camps@camanabay.com or call +1.345.640.2878.

    Cayman Music School Camp
    July 3-6, July 9-13 and July 16-20, 8 a.m.-2:30 p.m.
    Cayman Music School
    Throughout the month of July, Cayman Music School will be hosting a music camp for children ages 5-12. The camp will feature music, singing, dance, drama, arts and crafts, yoga and karaoke. The camp runs from 8 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. and the costs are as follows:
    July 3-6, CI$200; July 9-13, CI$250 and July 16-20, CI$250. Daily rates are CI$55 per child. For more information and to register your child, call +1.345.938.3838 or email info@caymanmusicschool.com.

    Classics at the Cinema
    Tuesdays throughout July, 7 p.m., Camana Bay Cinema
    Camana Bay Cinema features a classic movie every Tuesday as part of its Classics at the Cinema series. Shown in the VIP theatre, tickets are CI$20 per person and include a small popcorn. For more details, visit bigscreen.ky/classics.

    *July 3: Blazing Saddles (R)
    *July 10: The Mummy (1932, PG)
    *July 17: Top Gun (PG)
    *July 24: North by Northwest (PG)

    *Movies are subject to change.

    Camana Bay Cinema’s Kids Club
    Saturdays throughout July, 10 a.m., Camana Bay Cinema
    Kids and adults can take in classic family-friendly movies with brighter lights and softer sounds every Saturday. Tickets are CI$5 per person. For more details, visit bigscreen.ky/kids-club.

    *July 7: Cars 2 (G)
    *July 14: Inside Out (PG)
    *July 21: Cinderella (PG)
    *July 28: Tinkerbell and the Pirate Ferry (G)

    *Movies are subject to change.

    Budding Chef Summer Culinary Camp
    Tuesday, July 3-9, 10-11 a.m.
    Monday, July 23-27, 10-11 a.m., Bon Vivant
    Budding chefs ages 4-7 will learn how to make a different recipe each week. The cost is CI$35 per child per class and includes lunch, lessons and recipes. For more information and to register, call +1.345.623.2665 or email info@bonvivant.ky.

    Budding Chef Tween Summer Culinary Camp
    Tuesday, July 3-9, 12-1:30 p.m.
    Monday, July 23-27, 12-1:30 p.m., Bon Vivant
    Budding chefs ages 8-12 will learn how to prepare a different meal every day and will include lunch, recipes tours and lots of fun. The cost is CI$35 per child per class and includes lunch, lessons and recipes. For more information and to register, call +1.345.623.2665 or email info@bonvivant.ky.

    Summer Moonlight & Movies
    Tuesdays from July 10, 7-9 p.m., the Crescent
    Families can spend quality time together every Tuesday this summer and watch a different movie under the stars. Dine at one of Camana Bay’s signature restaurants before the movie, set up a picnic on the grass, or just bring a blanket, relax and enjoy the show on our outdoor big screen! This event is free and open to the public. Food and beverage available for purchase.

    *July 10: Zootopia (PG)
    *July 17: The Little Mermaid (G)
    *July 24: The Lion King (G)
    *July 31: Big Hero 6 (PG)

    *Movies subject to change.

    Shutterbugs Camp
    Monday, July 9 & 16, 9 a.m.-12 p.m. for ages 8-11
    Friday, July 13 & Monday, July 16, 9 a.m.-12 p.m., for ages 11-16 Picture This Studios
    Shutterbugs will embark on a photographic tour around the beautiful grounds of Camana Bay. Aspiring photographers will learn how to take care of their camera and explore the secrets of taking great photos. Kids must have their own camera, charged battery and memory card, a hat, sunscreen and a water bottle. The cost is CI$45 per child and spaces are limited. To reserve your space, call +1.345.943.3886 or email admin@picturethis.ky.

    The Bookends Club
    Wednesday, July 11, 6:30-7:30 p.m., Books & Books
    Join The Bookends Club when they meet to review their latest pick, LaRose by Louise Erdrich. Come share your insights and impressions in an open discussion. The Bookends Club gathers on the second Wednesday of every month and new members are always welcome.

    Lustic Life Book Club
    Thursday, July 12, 6 p.m., West Indies Wine Company
    Sophie Prior of Lustic Life invites you to the Lustic Life Book Club, happening every month at West Indies Wine Company. Each month’s book choice will be available for purchase at a discount of 20% off at Books & Books. This month’s book is Essentialism by Greg McKeown. This interactive class encourages readers to jot down their favourite quotes and passages and bring them to the discussion. To register, email sophie@lusticlife.com.

    World Cup Final
    Sunday, July 15, 9 a.m., the Town Centre
    Come out to the Crescent and cheer on your favourite football team in the finals. This event will showing at several cafes and eateries in the Town Centre. For more details, visit CamanaBay.com/events.

    Infinite Mindcare Talk Series
    Saturday, July 21, 2-3 p.m., Books & Books
    Books & Books hosts this monthly interactive dialogue promoting awareness on different topics about mental health with the experts from Infinite Mindcare. This month’s topic is Chill Out! Manage Your Anger Before It Manages You. For information on this event, call +1.345.640.2665.

    Culture at the Cinema
    Saturday, July 21, 8 p.m., Camana Bay Cinema
    Enjoy a pre-recorded screening of Macbeth, in the VIP theatre. Tickets are CI$40 per person. Must be 18 years or older to attend. For more information and tickets, visit bigscreen.ky/cultureschedule.

    10th Annual Camana Bay Basketball Camp 2018
    Monday, July 23-27, 8:30 a.m.-4 p.m., Arts & Recreation Centre
    The Camana Bay Basketball Camp celebrates 10 successful years this summer! Aspiring hoop stars can join the fun and train with professional basketball player, NBA star Gary Harris of the Denver Nuggets. Morning sessions run from 8:30 a.m. to 12 p.m. for ages seven to 12. Ages 12-17 attend afternoon sessions from 12:30 to 4 p.m. (12-year-old registrants can choose which session they wish to participate in based on their confidence and skill levels.) To register, email camps@camanabay.com or call 345.640.2878. The first 100 registrants receive a free gift!

    WEEKLY RITUALS

    Toddler Tuesdays
    Tuesdays: Imagination Playground 10 a.m.-7 p.m., the Crescent; and Camana Bay Story Time 11-11:30 a.m., Camana Bay Cinema
    Children can enjoy splashing in the interactive fountains at Imagination Playground and take part in a live story reading and sing along with exciting new books and beloved children’s songs with Camana Bay Story Time. These events are free and open to the public.

    Farmers & Artisans Market
    Wednesdays, 12-7 p.m., the Paseo
    Discover an array of local purveyors offering products and experiences found only in Cayman. Come early for the widest selection of fresh produce and farm goods then browse an eclectic selection of artisanal products and handicrafts.

    Open Canvas
    Wednesdays, 7 p.m., KARoo
    Every Wednesday, Open Canvas provides a public venue for local artists to display their talents and work on their latest creations. Art enthusiasts can join the gathering and watch paintings come to life one brushstroke at a time. Artists can contact douglascameron@cib.ky to reserve an easel and must bring their own art supplies. For more details, call +1.345.946.6633.

    The Flavour Tour
    Wednesdays, 7-9 p.m., West Indies Wine Company
    Savour the flavours of island life on the Camana Bay Flavour Tour! Join a Camana Bay host on a guided walking tour of our signature restaurants. Dine on small-plate courses featuring local ingredients presented in the unique styles of the participating restaurants. Begin the evening with a West Indies Wine Company wine card to enjoy the shop’s wines on tap, then experience our dining scene as your host takes you on a guided dining adventure of three Camana Bay restaurants for tantalising fare made with local ingredients. Finish off the night with a scoop of delicious sorbetto or gelato at Gelato & Co., featuring classic and new Italian creations as well as locally infused flavours, such as passionfruit and mango. The cost is US$89 per person all-inclusive. To purchase tickets, visit camanabay.com/flavour-tour. To enquire about private group bookings, please email flavourtour@camanabay.com for additional information.

    Friday Night Live Music
    Fridays, 7-10 p.m., Abacus
    Enjoy contemporary Caribbean sounds at Abacus with live entertainment on Friday evenings. Stop by for drinks before catching a movie or stay for dinner and welcome your weekend in style. For more details, call +1.345.623.8282.

    Imagination Playground
    Saturdays, 10 a.m.-7 p.m., the Crescent
    Imagination Playground invites kids to build, dream and explore a new world every week in the Town Centre’s dancing fountains with giant foam blocks in all shapes and sizes. Little ones can recreate familiar objects like animals, rocket ships and more. This event is every Tuesday and Saturday and is free and open to the public.

    Sing Along Story Time
    Saturdays, 10:30 a.m., Books & Books
    Young readers four to seven years old and their parents can join the storytellers at Books & Books to sing along to some of their favourite books.

    Saturday Night Live Music
    Saturdays, 7-10 p.m., Abacus
    Enjoy relaxing tunes performed live from 7-10 p.m. as you indulge in a tapas menu and drinks or treat yourself to a dinner date. For more details, call +1.345.623.8282.

    Sunday BBQ Brunch
    Sundays, 11 a.m.-3 p.m., KARoo
    Savour a tasty selection of fresh off-the-grill favourites with all the traditional BBQ fixings for CI$30 per person and CI$12.50 for children ages 12 and under. For more details, call +1.345.946.6633.

    Dim Sum Brunch
    Sundays, 11:30 a.m.-3 p.m., Mizu Asian Bistro + Bar
    Enjoy an expanded selection of dim sum and more at Mizu Asian Bistro + Bar’s Dim Sum Brunch every Sunday. Indulge in a selection of appetisers, soups and sushi with an array of noodle, rice and main dishes. The cost is CI$49 per person all-inclusive and reservations are required at +1.345.640.0001.

    –END–
