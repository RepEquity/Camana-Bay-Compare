    Hello summer! It’s time for fun in the sun, fireworks, live music, movies under the moonlight, shopping deals and kids’ camps. And of course you can’t forget about frozen treats and fountain fun, backyard grilling, boating, family time and summer vacations. These are the ingredients for the perfect summer!
    KIDS' CAMPS

    Cayman Music School Camp
    2-5 July, 8-12 July and 15-19 July (Ages 5-11)
    Cayman Music School
    For kids that want to tune their vocal cords or explore their creative side this summer, this camp is for them. Campers will enjoy activities such as lessons on singing, music, drama, dance and performance, and take part in karaoke fun as well as arts and crafts. The camp runs for three weeks and costs are as follows:
    2-5 July – CI$200
    8-12 July – $250
    15-19 July – $250
    To register, call 345.938.3838 or email info@caymanmusicschool.com.

    9th Annual Multi-Sport Camp
    2-5 July (Week 1), 8-12 July (Week 2) and 15-19 July (Week 3)
    8 a.m. to 12 p.m., Camana Bay Sports Complex
    The Multi-Sport Camp is a great way to keep little ones active this summer. Running for three weeks, this camp gives young athletes of all levels a chance to improve their fitness and sports skills, all while having a great time. Campers will be exposed to a variety of sports such as volleyball, basketball, tennis, swimming, football and more. The camp is open to kids ages 6 to 14 and costs CI$125 for Week 1 and $150 for Weeks 2 and 3. To register, email camps@camanabay.com or call 345.640.2878.

    11th Annual Basketball Camp
    22-26 July, 8:30 a.m. to 12 p.m. (Ages 7-12)
    and 12:30 p.m. to 4 p.m. (Ages 12-17)
    Arts & Recreation Centre
    Aspiring hoop stars can bring their A-game while receiving authentic NBA instruction and getting the opportunity to meet and train with a special guest from the NBA. Campers can improve their skills through drills and live games. The cost is CI$200 per person. To register, call 345.640.2878 or email camps@camanabay.com.

    3 Girls & A Kiln Kids’ Mini Camp
    5 August, 7 August and 9 August (Ages 9-14), 9 a.m. – 12 p.m.
    3 Girls & A Kiln
    This three-day mini camp will explore ceramics techniques, mosaic making, ink tiles and lots of fun mini art projects. Campers will have four projects to bring home over the three days. The cost is CI$150 per child, $10 discount for siblings and includes all materials and snacks. Spaces are limited. To register, email 3girlsandakiln@gmail.com.

    EVENTS

    Moonlight & Movies
    16 July, 7 p.m., the Crescent
    Enjoy quality family time as you watch the movie “How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World” (PG, 2019) under the stars. Food and drink can be purchased at any of the Camana Bay merchants, or feel free to set up a picnic on the grass, blanket and all! The event is free and open to the public.

    Christmas in July
    25 – 26 July, 3 Girls & A Kiln
    Forget the countdown, celebrate Christmas early, in July! During the evenings of 25-26 July enjoy a Christmas glazing class , a special Christmas-themed wood class, giveaways and sugar cookies! Ugly sweater’s welcomed! For more information call 345.325.8217 or email 3girlsandakiln@gmail.com.

    Summer Sidewalk Sale
    Saturday, 3 August from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.
    Shop and save this summer at Camana Bay! Enjoy special offers, discounts of up to 70% off, product displays and more at participating Camana Bay shops.

    Bon Vivant Kid’s Summer Cooking Series
     Three five-day series and two-hour classes available (no single-day enrollments):
    5 -9 August, 9 a.m. – 11 a.m. and 12 p.m. – 2 p.m., Ages 8-12
    5-9 August, 3 p.m. – 5 p.m. and 6 p.m. – 8 p.m., Ages 13-17
    12-16 August, 9 a.m. – 11 a.m. and 12 p.m. - 2 p.m., Ages 8-12
    12-16 August, 3 p.m. – 5 p.m. and 6 p.m. – 8 p.m., Ages 13-17
    19-23 August, 9 a.m. – 11 a.m. and 12 p.m. - 2 p.m., Ages 8-12
    19-23 August, 3.p.m. – 5 p.m. and 6 p.m. – 8 p.m., Ages 13-17

    Your growing chef will enjoy a full menu of delights and learn new culinary techniques, all crafted by chef Thomas Tennant of Tomfoodery Kitchen in this summer cooking series at Bon Vivant. Young chefs will get generous samples of each dish they make and will take home a packet of recipes and their apron. They’ll also get a chance to show off their skills at the end of the series with a friendly Mystery Box team competition. Classes run for about two hours per day and last for five consecutive days. The cost is CI$235 for all five days. For more information or to register call 345.623.2665 or email info@bonvivant.ky.
